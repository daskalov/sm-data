Fall term holiday schedule changes:
The normal Thursday schedule of course activities is cancelled for December 3, 2009. Classes are cancelled for all faculties except Dentistry, Medicine, Continuing Education (non-credit courses and English & French credit courses), Stage in Dietetics Level 3 and Student Teaching for Education students.
