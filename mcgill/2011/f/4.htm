Fall term holiday schedule changes:
The normal Thursday schedule of course activities is cancelled for December 3, 2009. In its place, all lectures, labs, conferences and other course-related activities that are normally held on Mondays will be held on Thursday, December 3, 2009 as well. This change in schedule is to make up for activities that are cancelled on Monday, October 12 due to Thanksgiving Day.

Winter term study break: February 22 to 26, 2010

Classes are cancelled for all faculties except Dentistry, Medicine, Continuing Education (non-credit courses and English & French credit courses), Stage in Dietetics Level 3 and Student Teaching for Education students.
